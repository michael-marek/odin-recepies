Kotlet Schabowy

Description

A kotlet is a type of fried meat popular all over Europe

Ingredients

  • Meat (usually chicken breast or pork)
  • 2+ eggs
  • flour
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Cut your meat into slices around 4cm thick. If you're using a chicken breast, use the whole thing
  2. Place your meat on a cutting board and cover with cling film
  3. Use a tenderiser to tenderise and flatten your Meat
  4. Pour a thin layer of oil onto a pan and heat to medium while you prepare the rest of the setup
  5. Season each tenderised slice with salt, pepper, and any other spice you like
  6. Crack your eggs into a bowl and beat them untill the yokes mix with the whites
  7. Pour both the flour and breadcrumbs onto indvidual plates
  8. Coat one slice into the flour, eggs, then breadcrumbs in that order
  9. Immediately put the slice into the oil
  10. Flip onto the other side after 2 minutes
  11. Repeat with the rest of your cutlets, replacing the oil after every 2-3 rounds
